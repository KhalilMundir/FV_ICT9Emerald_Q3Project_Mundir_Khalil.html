GORDON RAMSAY

Gordon Ramsay was born in Scotland and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England, where he hoped to be a professional player. When an injury ended his ambitions for a flourishing career on the pitch, Ramsay returned to college to complete a hotel management course. His persistence and natural aptitude led him to train alongside some of the world's best chefs.

Ramsay is now worldwide known, with seven Michelin stars, and has opened 34 restaurants in Europe, America, Asia, and the Middle East. Ramsay has also become a television sensation in the United States and the United Kingdom, receiving an Emmy nomination in 2017. Ramsay has published numerous books, many of which have been international blockbusters; his most recent cookbook is Gordon Ramsay's Quick and Delicious.
